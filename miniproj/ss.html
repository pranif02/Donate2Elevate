Senior Secondary books

The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) is an autonomous organization in India that develops and publishes textbooks for various subjects and classes. Here are the primary class textbooks of class 11 and 12 published by NCERT:

Class 11:
Physics: Physics Part I, Physics Part II
Chemistry: Chemistry Part I, Chemistry Part II
Biology: Biology
Mathematics: Mathematics
English: Hornbill (English Core), Snapshot (English Elective)


Class 12:
Physics: Physics Part I, Physics Part II
Chemistry: Chemistry Part I, Chemistry Part II
Biology: Biology
Mathematics: Mathematics Part I, Mathematics Part II
English: Flamingo (English Core), Vistas (English Elective)
